Cauliflower and asparagus salad

Ingredients:

  • cauliflower 300g
  • asparagus 250g
  • pumpkin 300g
  • zucchini 200g
  • olive oil 2 1 tbsp.
  • garlic powder
  • salt
  • pepper
  • thyme 100g
  • cider vinegar 1 tbsp.
  • sunflower seeds 2 tbsp.

Steps:

  1. Chop the cauliflower branches in a food processor, place them in a glass bowl, cover them and microwave them for 4 minutes at maximum power.
  2. Cut the base of the asparagus, wash them and cut them into slices, reserving the split ends.
  3. Cut the pumpkin into thin slices, wash the courgette and cut it into slices. Drizzle with half the oil, sprinkle with the garlic powder and stir gently.
  4. Heat the remaining oil in a large non-stick frying pan, add the split ends of the asparagus and sauté for 2 to 3 minutes.
  5. Place the remaining asparagus in the pan, add the pumpkin and courgette and cook over medium to high heat until the vegetables are tender but crispy.
  6. Season with salt, a little pepper and thyme leaves, fresh or dried. Drizzle with the vinegar and toss the vegetables with the cauliflower.
  7. Garnish with the split ends of the sautéed asparagus and sprinkle with the sunflower seeds.
