Pastel de nata

Ingredients:

  • milk 1L
  • lemon peels 2 uni.
  • cinnamon stick 2 uni.
  • sugar 500g
  • flour 100g
  • egg 2 eggs + 10 yolks 12 uni.
  • puff pastry 500g

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 240°C.
  2. In a saucepan, place the milk, lemon peels and cinnamon sticks.
  3. Bring to a boil until it boils and turn off.
  4. In a bowl, mix the sugar with the flour.
  5. Add the mixture to the milk, still hot, stirring well.
  6. Remove the lemon peels and cinnamon sticks and let cool for 10 minutes.
  7. Add the whole eggs and the yolks. Stir with the sticks until you form a homogeneous cream. Bring to the boil again, stirring constantly until thickened.
  8. Roll the puff pastry into a cylinder and cut into slices of 3 cm each.
  9. With the help of moistened thumbs, line small metallic molds, spreading the dough well to the edge.
  10. Pour the filling into the molds and bake for 15 minutes, or until golden. Serve while still hot. Suggestion: sprinkle with cinnamon.

Coffee chocolate brownie

Ingredients:

  • butter 60g
  • chopped dark chocolate 150g
  • sugar 270g
  • coffee grounds 4 tbsp.
  • eggs 2 uni.
  • cocoa powder 50g
  • flour tbsp.
  • salt
  • coffee 60ml

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C. Line a square shape (20cm x 20cm) with parchment paper.
  2. Place the butter in a bowl and melt it in the microwave. Add the chocolate and mix, giving the microwave enough time for the chocolate to melt. Wrap well and set aside.
  3. In a large bowl, mix the sugar with the coffee grounds. Gradually add the melted chocolate with the butter and mix, with the help of a mixer. Add one egg and then the other, and continue to mix. Add the cocoa, flour, salt and coffee and mix well, until everything is incorporated. Pour the mixture into the mold and bake in the oven for 30-35 minutes.
