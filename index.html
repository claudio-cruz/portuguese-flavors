Portuguese Gastronomy

Portugal is known for its rich gastronomy, a gastronomy rich of flavour, colour and history. Portuguese gastronomy has a long history and has been changing and evolving over the years. Nowadays Portugal offers a vast number of delicious gastronomic options both meat, fish, vegetarian and desserts for all tastes. In our website you can find diverse dishes throughout our different categories.

Portuguese gastronomy is known for its flavours and good quality ingredients. Portugal is located in the South of Europe in the entrance of the Mediterranean Sea. That geographical location provides excellent weather, abundant sunlight, good humidity, fertile soils and water abundance. Those conditions create the perfect scenario for growing big, delicious and nutritious vegetables and fruits. It also contributes for farming good livestock.
Most of the Portuguese border is coastline, bathed by the Atlantic ocean. An ocean rich in sea life. That factor, throughout the years, enriched the Portuguese gastronomy with high quality and a variety of fish dishes.
Portuguese people have a sweet tooth therefore, in Portuguese gastronomy you can find a lot of delicious and unique desserts.
